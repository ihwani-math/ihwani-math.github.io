Ivan L. Ihwani (盧斯非)

I am interested in problems in numerical methods, high-performance computing, and machine learning tuning for partial differential equations.

I have recently completed my PhD in Mathematics at the Department of Mathematics, National Central University (NCU), supervised by Prof. Feng-Nan Hwang (黃楓南).

I am also a teaching staff at Department of Mathematics, UGM .
