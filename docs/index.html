Harry Richman

I am interested in problems in tropical geometry (what's that??), phylogenetics, combinatorics, and number theory.

I am currently a postdoc at NCTS mentored by Liang-Chung Hsia (夏 良忠). I previously held postdoc positions in the Matsen group, part of the Herbold Computational Biology Program at the Fred Hutch Cancer Center, and at the University of Washington where my mentor was Farbod Shokrieh. Before that I was a graduate student at the University of Michigan where my advisors were David Speyer and Jeff Lagarias. I was on the academic job market in fall 2023.

Before graduate school I studied math at MIT and the Math in Moscow program.
